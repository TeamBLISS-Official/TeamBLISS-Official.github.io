Problem

  • Landfill with plastic bottle picture
  • In public spaces, people often discard partially consumed plastic beverage bottles, leading to recycling challenges because the separation of bottles and residual liquids is required for proper recycling.
  • Current methods include manual separation and complete disposal of the bottle. However, manual separation is inefficient and unsanitary, while complete disposal causes the plastic bottles to go directly into landfill, which not only harms the environment but also reduces the reuse of a valuable non-renewable resource, thus hindering the circular economy.
  • Shockingly, only 29% of plastic bottles were recycled in the United States in 2017 (Hyperlink https://www.cbc.ca/news/science/bottle-recycling-1.5416614). And currently there is no efficient, hygienic, and user-friendly technology in the market to address this issue, making our innovation a game-changer in the plastic bottle recycling industry.
