Video of the process from start to finish ?

Text below the video

  • When a water bottle is placed in the designated slot, the photoelectric sensor receives a signal.

  • This prompts the camera to initiate recognition and counting of the bottles, with the data displayed on the small LED screen in the front.

  • Once recognized, the robotic arm securely clamps the bottle body.

  • Simultaneously, the bottle cap twister spins to twist off the bottle cap.

  • Meanwhile, any residual water within the bottle drops down, accumulating in the water reservoir at the bottom.

  • Finally, the robotic arm swings above the bin and gently drops the bottle, completing the entire process.
