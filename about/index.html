About Us

BLISS (Bottle Liquid Separation System)

Our two co-founders, Xinyuan and Boyu, met online because of our similar interests in technology and innovation. We are both determined highschoolers who are dedicated to solving environmental related issues using our technological skills. We observed that people often throw away plastic bottles containing water, which might cause problems in the recycling process, so we decided to design a device to solve this problem.

We believe that while conceptual ideas hold important value, their practical application is crucial for tangible impact. Determined to create positive impact on society using our idea, we have transitioned from mere ideation to concrete action by establishing a startup focused on bringing the concepts of BLISS to fruition.
